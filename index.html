Start of round.
[PHASE ONE]

* At the start of round the one with the best B spawn takes awp and goes to the left side of tunnel to try and get an early frag on a T peeking early.

* The other closest player will be there to support him in case of tunnel rush and / or from lower.

* Orange watches cross and if too many ct's cross everyone in tunnels go lower to go short with green who is already in mid.

*Green, makes his way mid. On the left side of the wall as to not get shot by the mid awper. Keeping crosshair on short.

*Blue goes in to long doors waiting to punish the potential push.

It's very important that nobody over extends their positions in this phase

PHASE 2.

*Phase 2 commences once purple gets a pick on B. Yellow rushes while purple flashes and follows him

(don't mind the icons)

As yellow and purple get site orange flashes over mid doors to blind any ct watching it while green goes through to catch the ct blind. Once he does, green or orange should smoke ct to safely cross to B.
(If Purple+Yellow get site early they can peek mid to help green and orange get the mid player)

(couldn't remove icons-lel)

Blue moves out of long doors and on to cat, walking. To catch any ct rotating off guard. Rotate to B through lowers if you get the elbow player.

PHASE 3

Phase 3 commences once B is cleared.
Purple and Yellow move to their respective waypoints on the image left to support Orange and Green while they cross CT.
